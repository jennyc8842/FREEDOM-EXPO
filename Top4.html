Top 4 V.R sets

repl.it

There are many companies that make the VR headsets that are way better. Here are some of the top 4 best-selling ones.

The first one is the Value Index.







Pros

  • Widest field of view
  • Great knuckle controllers

Cons

  • Needs a high-end GPU
  • Very expensive




The second best selling one is HTC Vive.

Pros

  • Best VR experiences
  • Partnered with a good software company, Value

Cons

  • Needs a high-end GPU
  • Very pricey


The third selling one is called the Sony PlayStation VR.

Pros

  • Affordable
  • Computer level performance
  • Variety of games

Cons

  • Problems with sealing lights
  • Motion controllers are not appealing


Lastly, the 4th best selling headset is the Oculus Rift.

Pros

  • Fits comfortablely around your head
  • Awesome selection of games
  • Long list of apps and movies

Cons

  • Dizziness
  • Needs a lot of requirements in PC
